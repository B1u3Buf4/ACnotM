问题描述 :

It is an interesting problem from this website: http://www.******.ru(Will be published after this contest!).

Here is a electron plate, the formula displayed in the plate was correct before one of the pixels being broken, and only one of the pixels was broken, so that the formula is wrong now. Your task is very easy, just output the original formula.
I will show you the format in the sample output.

Remember ,what you should do is : change a pixel’s state in the sample output and make the formula correct.Then , output the correct formula.
